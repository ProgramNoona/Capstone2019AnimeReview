About Page

AniRater is an anime review site created by five students engaged in their Capstone project. It is the result of a lot of hours of hard work, late nights, and desperately wishing we could actually all meet at the same time to put it all together. Sickness, car accidents, cat bites, and plain ol' sleeping in has taken its toll on the project. However, through the power of persistence, it is what you see here.

This website was created in the first half of 2019, during the spring semester at Fayetteville Technical Community College. Its purpose is to create a sense of community in discussing anime together. Or just to help us pass the class and graduate with relatively good grades.
